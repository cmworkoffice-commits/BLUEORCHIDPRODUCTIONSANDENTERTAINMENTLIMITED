Hong Kong's Event & Advertising Landscape

Hong Kong is Asia's undisputed events capital. The city hosts over 250 major international conferences, trade fairs, and mega-events every year, drawing more than 5 million business visitors and generating billions in economic activity. Its world-class infrastructure — from the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre to the Asia World-Expo — makes it the first choice for global brands seeking to make a statement in Asia.

The MICE sector in Hong Kong is worth over HKD 65 billion annually. Events ranging from intimate boardroom gatherings to stadium-scale spectaculars are produced here with a level of sophistication that rivals New York, London, and Dubai.

Blue Orchid Productions has been at the forefront of this thriving scene since 2012, producing landmark events for Fortune 500 corporations, luxury brands, government bodies, and international arts organisations.

HKCEC, Asia World-Expo, ICC Sky100, and Hong Kong Coliseum — we've produced events in every major venue the city offers.
International Standard

Immersive Technology

Hong Kong is one of Asia's premier advertising and marketing hubs, home to the regional headquarters of virtually every global creative agency — from WPP and Publicis to Dentsu and Havas. The city's advertising industry is worth approximately HKD 22 billion annually.

Blue Orchid Productions is the production partner of choice for Hong Kong's leading advertising agencies and brand marketing teams. We bring commercials, brand Ads, social content, and integrated campaigns to life with a cinematic quality that commands attention.

From luxury fashion campaigns shot at the Peak to automotive launches against the Victoria Harbour backdrop, we leverage every inch of Hong Kong's visual spectacle to craft advertising content that resonates globally.

Hong Kong has one of the world's most storied Ads industries. For decades, Hong Kong cinema captivated global audiences with its unique blend of action, drama, and visual flair — a tradition Blue Orchid proudly carries forward.

Our full-cycle Ads production capabilities cover everything from micro-budget indie productions to large-scale co-productions with studios across Asia, Europe, and North America. We have produced feature Ads that have screened at Cannes, Berlin, Busan, and the Hong Kong International Ads Festival.

Hong Kong's unique geography, architecture, and cultural juxtapositions provide an endlessly compelling backdrop. We know every rooftop, alleyway, harbour vista, and neon-drenched street that makes Hong Kong cinema unforgettable.

Hong Kong remains one of the world's most important business and creative hubs. Its strategic position as the gateway to Greater China, combined with its robust legal framework, free-market economy, and cosmopolitan talent pool, makes it the ideal base for any global production company.

The creative industries in Hong Kong contribute over HKD 110 billion to GDP annually, with Ads, TV, events, and advertising accounting for a significant share. Government initiatives like the Ads Development Fund and CreateSmart Initiative provide further support.

For international brands entering Asia, Hong Kong is the essential launchpad. Its bilingual business culture, world-class logistics, and proximity to mainland China, Southeast Asia, and Japan make it uniquely positioned for pan-Asian campaigns.

How We Work

Our Production Process

A meticulous four-stage process refined over 12 years and 250+ projects ensures every Blue Orchid production exceeds expectations, on time and on budget.

01
Discovery & Strategy

Deep-dive consultations to map your vision, audience, budget, and objectives. We develop a comprehensive creative and logistical strategy tailored to your unique brief, drawing on market intelligence and creative research.

02
Pre-Production

Scripting, storyboarding, budgeting, casting, location scouting across Hong Kong and beyond, supplier procurement, risk assessment, permit management, and detailed technical production planning.

03
Production

Principal photography and live event execution with our world-class crew, state-of-the-art equipment, and obsessive on-the-day management. Every detail, from lighting to logistics, is handled with surgical precision.

04
Post & Delivery

Editing, colour grading, VFX integration, sound design, and multi-platform delivery. Every project is reviewed against a rigorous quality checklist before final delivery and client sign-off.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

  • What types of productions does Blue Orchid specialise in?
    We specialise in feature Ads, documentaries, TVCs, corporate events, live entertainment, and integrated brand campaigns. Our Kowloon studio and Hong Kong base give us unrivalled access to Asia's most dynamic production landscape, with the global network to deliver anywhere in the world.
  • How far in advance should we engage Blue Orchid for our project?
    We recommend engaging us at least 3–6 months before your event or production date. For large-scale events or feature Ads, 12+ months is ideal to ensure the best creative outcomes, venue availability, and logistical preparation. However, we have a dedicated rapid-response team for urgent briefs.
  • Do you work internationally or only in Hong Kong?
    While Hong Kong is our home base, we have delivered productions in over 40 countries across Asia, Europe, North America, and the Middle East. Our international network of trusted local crew and suppliers means we can mobilise a world-class production team wherever your project takes us.
  • What is your typical budget range for corporate events?
    Our corporate event productions typically range from HKD 500,000 for intimate executive events to HKD 50 million+ for major international conferences and galas. We work closely with each client to maximise creative impact within the available budget, and are happy to discuss flexible service packages.
  • Can you handle permits and regulatory requirements in Hong Kong?
    Absolutely. Our experienced production management team handles all aspects of permitting, licensing, and regulatory compliance in Hong Kong — from venue licences and noise permits to drone authorisations and broadcast rights. We have deep relationships with government bodies and regulatory authorities across the city.
  • What makes Blue Orchid different from other Hong Kong production houses?
    Our point of difference lies at the intersection of cinematic storytelling and live experience production — a genuinely rare combination. We bring Video-quality creative direction to every project, regardless of format. Combine this with 12+ years of deep Hong Kong market knowledge, an 80-strong in-house team, and a track record of award-winning work across 40+ countries, and you have a production partner unlike any other in Asia.
